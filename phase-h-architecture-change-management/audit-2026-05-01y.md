### F4. quality-report.py — pre-prod share + 30-day trend

Predicate: P11. Path:
[`scripts/test-runners/quality-report.py`](../scripts/test-runners/quality-report.py).

**Symptom.** Walks `phase-h/audit-*.md` for FAIL + WARN findings
(pre-prod catches), `phase-g/postmortems.md` for `***`-separated
stories (incidents), computes `pre_prod_share` over a configurable
rolling window. Output at this commit (365-day window — short window
yields n/a until enough audit walks land):

```
- pre-prod catches: 28 (FAIL + WARN audit findings)
- incidents:        14 (postmortems entries)
- pre_prod_share:   0.667  (28 / 42)
```

Band: italian-strike (≥ 0.6 < 0.8). Architect-velocity / R&D-throughput
goals see the cost; this is the trade.

**Note.** The 0.667 baseline is the actual signal: 2/3 of bugs are
caught pre-prod via the audit cycle + test runners; 1/3 hit prod
(and land in postmortems). Trend over the next month is the metric
ADR 0021 was created to drive — incidents should slow down as the
9 quality ADRs land their `*Taken:*`-line preventions in production
runners.

(g, h) or "22 broken links" (audit-2026-04-30 initial). The

paragraphs of earlier audit findings (the auditor was correctly
documenting *what was broken*, and the broken-pattern text is
still part of the historical record but not a live nav link).

**Note.** The 4 actually-broken live links are in F1 above. The
other 12 fixes in F1 Category A came from a more permissive
sweep that treated some valid-looking but stale targets (e.g.,
the `phase-d/architecture.md` `../adr/…` paths) as broken even
though they were already wrong before the inflated count was
recorded. Net: the working tree is now 0/0.

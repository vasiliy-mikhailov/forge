### F1. F11 closed via ADR 0013 dec 9 (transitive-coverage carve-out)

Predicate: P5. Path:
`phase-preliminary/adr/0013-md-as-source-code-tdd.md`.

**Symptom.** ADR 0013 now contains decision point 9, *Transitive
coverage of single-role process specs*: when a process spec md is
exclusively activated from a single role's persona file, the
role's tests transitively cover the process spec; no separate
`tests/<process-spec-path>` md is required, provided the role md
*names* the transitively-covered process spec.

**Closure.** Two process specs qualify under dec 9 today:

- `phase-requirements-management/wiki-requirements-collection.md`
  is loaded only by [Wiki PM](../phase-b-business-architecture/roles/wiki-pm.md);
  the role's "Tests" section now explicitly names it as
  transitively covered.
- `phase-h-architecture-change-management/audit-process.md` is
  loaded only by [Auditor](../phase-b-business-architecture/roles/auditor.md);
  the role's "Tests" section names it likewise.

The carve-out is principled (not a test-skipping shortcut): if
a process spec has multiple activation paths or is invoked
free-standing, dec 9 does NOT apply and the canonical
`tests/.../test-<name>.md` is still mandatory.

### F2. P14 spec needed a carve-out for persona instances; closed in same commit

Predicate: P14. Path:
`phase-h-architecture-change-management/audit-process.md`.

**Symptom.** The 5 persona files under `roles/customers/` lack
the canonical `## Motivation chain` / `## Purpose` / `## Activates
from` sections that P14 expected of every file under
`phase-b-business-architecture/roles/`. Per ADR 0016, personas
are *instances* of the abstract Wiki Customer role —
motivation chain is uniform across instances and lives in the
abstract role; per-persona files document reading-mode + pain
signature + severity calibration (which IS the persona-specific
motivation, in domain-specific form). Forcing per-persona
motivation chains would be N-fold duplication of identical
content.

**Note.** P14 spec updated in this commit with a carve-out per
ADR 0013 dec 9 (transitive coverage):

- `roles/customers/` — persona instances; motivation chain in
  the abstract `wiki-customer.md`.
- `roles/collaborations/` — composes member roles' motivation
  chains; no separate chain required.

The carve-out is principled (single source of truth per ADR
0015 dec 1) — not a test-skipping shortcut. Future role files
under `roles/` (other than the two carved-out subdirectories)
still require their own motivation chain.

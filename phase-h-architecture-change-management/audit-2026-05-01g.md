### F1. P20 single-hit findings on 6 operational md files

Predicate: P20. Paths (each: 1 `repeated-title` hit on the file's
own H1 appearing once as plain text in the body):

- `phase-f-migration-planning/migration-plan.md`
- `phase-h-architecture-change-management/trajectory-model.md`
- `phase-preliminary/architecture-method.md`
- `phase-preliminary/architecture-repository.md`
- `phase-preliminary/architecture-team.md`
- `phase-requirements-management/wiki-requirements-collection.md`

**Symptom.** Each file's H1 title (e.g. `# Architecture method`)
appears once as plain text in the body, typically in a definition
pointer pattern like *"forge's chosen architecture method"* or
*"the Level 1 / Level 2 trajectory model"*. The pattern is at the
boundary between bloat (restated title-as-filler) and signal
(definition pointer in opening prose). 1 hit each → WARN, not
FAIL; per P20 a file becomes FAIL only at ≥ 3 hits.

**Rule.** [P20 in audit-process.md](audit-process.md);
[Driver "Token-count bloat"](../phase-a-architecture-vision/drivers.md).

**Proposed fix or escalation.** Each file: rewrite the one
restating sentence to elide the title (e.g. *"forge's chosen
method, declared here in lieu of inheriting one wholesale"* —
drop the redundant *"architecture"* before *"method"*; the H1 is
3 lines above and the reader has the context). Mechanical edit;
deferred to a P20 cleanup sweep.

       cross-reference, not a title restatement. Eliminated 1
